The Sai Sword.

It is a sword said to have been crafted in the past, accompanied by rituals, specifically timed to the Pure Yang of the tiger (In)—the Year of the Tiger, the Month of the Tiger, the Day of the Tiger, and the Hour of the Tiger—to repel ghosts.

In particular, the sword currently held in the hand of the Team Leader of Hyunmoo Team 3 from the Disaster Management Bureau was one made entirely for ritual purposes, with an unsharpened blade.

However, for that very reason, it exerts unparalleled power against supernatural disasters.

"Hup."

Naturally, it cannot be used in every supernatural disaster.

Nor can just anyone use it.

—A situation where a wicked and corrupt thing, in the form of a disaster or a ghost, is directly invading.

The sword’s true power only manifests when these conditions are met as closely as possible.

However, in supernatural disasters, one usually enters or gets caught up in an Anomaly's territory.

Conversely, cases where the opponent 'invades' are rare, and there are not many instances where they are judged as completely corrupt.

However.

'It’s perfect.'

The Team Leader's hand trembles with something like a thrill.

Because the target had invaded the area where she herself was working as an employee.

And because it had even shed its mask of a friendly mascot of the theme park!

Every condition clicked together like a puzzle, to the point of feeling euphoria.

"Ha!"

Thus, the Pure Yang energy of the sword, surging through half of the Sword Art, becomes a thunderbolt and strikes the corrupt and wicked thing.

With unparalleled strength.

Completely!

Aaaaaagh!

"Ack!!"

"...!!"

And in the next moment.

Rumble, crash!!

Completely.
