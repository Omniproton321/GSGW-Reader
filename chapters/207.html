If Kim Soleum had successfully escaped, there was a high probability she would be a nuisance, and it was a dangerous attempt that might even reveal her own identity.

‘...I feel like I’m being suspected of being a spy too.’

She didn’t know.

At any rate, unlike the feeling of a spy walking on thin ice, there was no particular change in the agents’ attitudes.

And she hadn’t been suddenly dragged to the Glass Prison either.

Out of the three who joined, one was isolated due to Contamination, and one was missing.

She was the only one left, and it was realistically close to impossible for a rookie alone to steal World-ending Class Disaster documents.

And she had to do what needed to be done.

The subway of the Abyss Transit Authority.
